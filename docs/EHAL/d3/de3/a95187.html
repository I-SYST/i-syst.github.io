Detailed Description

TphSensor implementation class of TE Connectivity's MS8607-02BA01 PHT Combination Sensor.

The MS8607 is the novel digital combination sensor of MEAS providing 3 environmental physical measurements all-in-one: pressure, humidity and temperature (PHT).

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Integrated pressure, humidity and temperature sensor
  • QFNpackage5x3x1mm3
  • Operating range: 10 to 2000 mbar, 0RH to 100RH, -40 to 85 °C
  • High-resolution module: 0.016 mbar, 0.04RH, 0.01°C
  • Supply voltage: 1.5 to 3.6 V
  • Fully factory calibrated sensor
  • I2C interface
Examples:
TPHSensorTag.cpp.
